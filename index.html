KICK BACK & JOIN US

This year for the 3rd Annual Learning & Innovation Summit, we've decided to take a slightly different approach to shake things up a bit in our educational. We know there is a lot of necessary online professional development going on and that the options for large online conferences are many. So we decided we are not going to do a big online conference.

We are offering a free, 2 hour opportunity to take a peek into another teacher's online classroom. How is it organized? What is the schedule? What's been working? If you have ever watched MTV's Cribs back in the day, that's what we want to do. . .only Ed Tech Style. We are showcasing online classrooms. Each of two morning sessions will offer a brief glimpse into different online classrooms by some stellar teachers. We will focus on organization, not necessarily the platform or tools.

You don't have to do anything. Just kick back and listen or watch. No dressing up, no need to turn on your video. Just grab some coffee or your favorite morning drink and find a comfortable spot to kick back and let someone else do the heavy thinking for a while.

We are a collaborative team focused on accelerating, scaling, and amplifying the number and speed of innovations coming from the San Diego County Office of Education (SDCOE). We provide support to district and school leaders, staff, students, and the greater San Diego community to translate ideas into impact and generate new solutions at every level.
